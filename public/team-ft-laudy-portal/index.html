ALWAYS a decline

  • Currently taking PAIN PILLS for ongoing pain
  • Any history of INSULIN / DIABETES Rx
  • Any history of BLOOD THINNERS Rx
  • Any history of Bipolar / Schizophrenia Rx
  • Any history of Drug / Alcohol Addiction Rx
  • Any history of Arthritis Rx (NOT over-the-counter)
  • Any history of Overactive / Underactive Bladder Rx
  • History of SEIZURES taking over 1,200 mg of daily meds

High blood pressure rules

  • Height & weight must be WITHIN the HBP range (see Height & Weight chart)
  • Must be taking the same med for OVER A YEAR
  • OKAY to take a water pill with HBP meds

HBP declines: listing sleep apnea with HBP on the app · taking MORE than 2 HBP meds (not counting a water pill) · older clients (50+) who smoke and take HBP meds.

Mental health tips

  • ADHD meds are OKAY for dependents

Mental-health declines: listing a DEPENDENT's history of anxiety or depression Rx · currently taking TWO meds for depression OR anxiety · currently taking depression or anxiety meds AND telling the V-Call they are currently in therapy.
